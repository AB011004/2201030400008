Cardiovascular Training Workout

Warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Light cardio (e.g., jogging, jumping jacks)
  • Dynamic stretches (e.g., arm circles, leg swings)

Main Workout (30-45 minutes):

Activity Duration Intensity Rest
Running or Brisk Walking 20 minutes Moderate 1 minute
Interval Training (e.g., sprinting for 30 seconds, walking for 60 seconds) 3 sets of 4 minutes each High 1 minute rest between sets
Stationary Bike or Elliptical Trainer 15 minutes Moderate-High 1 minute

Cool-down (5-10 minutes):

  • Light cardio (e.g., walking, slow jogging)
  • Static stretches (e.g., hamstring stretch, quad stretch)
