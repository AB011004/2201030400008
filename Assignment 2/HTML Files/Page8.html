Fat Loss Workout

Warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Light cardio (e.g., jogging, jumping jacks, jumping rope)
  • Dynamic stretches (e.g., arm circles, leg swings, torso twists)

Main Workout (30-45 minutes):

Note: This is a circuit workout. Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, with 15 seconds rest between exercises. Complete the entire circuit 2-3 times, depending on your fitness level.

Exercise Focus
Jumping jacks Full body
Squats (modify with bodyweight or weights) Legs and core
Push-ups (modification: incline push-ups, decline push-ups, knee push-ups) Chest, shoulders, triceps
Mountain climbers Core and legs
Burpees Full body
High knees Cardio and core
Lunge jumps Legs and core
Plank Core
Rest -

Cool-down (5-10 minutes):

  • Light cardio (e.g., walking, slow jogging)
  • Static stretches (e.g., hamstring stretch, quad stretch, chest stretch)
