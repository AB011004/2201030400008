Strength Training Workout

Warm-up (5 minutes):

  • Light cardio (e.g., jogging, jumping jacks)
  • Dynamic stretches (e.g., arm circles, leg swings)

Main Workout (30-45 minutes):

Exercise Sets Reps Rest
Squats 3 8-12 30 seconds
Push-ups 3 Max reps 30 seconds
Rows (bodyweight or with weights) 3 8-12 30 seconds
Lunges 3 10-15 per leg 30 seconds
Plank 3 30 seconds hold 30 seconds rest

Cool-down (5-10 minutes):

  • Static stretches (e.g., hamstring stretch, quad stretch)
  • Foam rolling (optional)
